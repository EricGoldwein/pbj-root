# PBJ320 Premium workflows (counsel and investigators)

Distilled from PBJ320 Premium counsel guidance. This is **not legal advice**. Premium combines PBJ daily staffing, Provider Information, and optional Employee Detail in one facility-scoped dashboard.

## Before you paste into AI

1. **Verify CCN** in the header matches pleadings, Form 2567, and Care Compare.
2. **Match dates** to your operative period. PBJ is work-date based; quarters follow CMS reporting.
3. **Note provenance** — data vintage and dashboard date — before sharing outside the team.
4. **Cite CMS datasets** in filings; use the dashboard as a structured view. Definitions: https://www.pbj320.com/premium/methods

## Reasonable section walkthrough (Premium dashboard)

1. **Summary** — census, hours, HPRD, headline indicators.
2. **Compliance** — federal minimums and state rules where loaded; legal conclusions remain yours.
3. **Benchmarks** — peer and geography context; document comparator logic if challenged.
4. **Trends** — longitudinal staffing, contract mix, day-of-week patterns, pre/post windows.
5. **Employee tracker** — when present: position/employer rollups from CMS Employee Detail (facility-reported).
6. **Daily PBJ** — work-date drill-down; capture filters and CCN on exhibits.
7. **Red flags** — screening views for unusual days — **hypothesis generators, not proof** of substandard care.
8. **Methodology** — on-report definitions; cross-check https://www.pbj320.com/premium/methods

## Example use cases

- **Incident week:** Filter Daily PBJ to the incident week; compare direct HPRD and contract share to prior 30 work days. Export with date range, CCN, and category definitions.
- **Ownership change:** Bracket quarters in Trends; run pre/post windows aligned to allegations.
- **Expert outline:** Summary and Benchmarks for narrative; Compliance and Trends for timelines; Daily PBJ for specific dates.

## Discovery angles

- Quarter tie-out: PBJ rollups vs payroll or cost reports by quarter and category.
- Contract staffing: agency hours if relevant to notice, adequacy, or stability theories.
- Chain context: PBJ320 entity views for portfolio patterns beyond one CCN.

## Exhibit hygiene

- Include URL, date accessed, CCN, and active filters on exhibit lists or declarations.
- Prefer citing **CMS primary datasets**; attach Methods definitions for HPRD and staff categories.

## Limitations (Premium)

- Staffing and census are **facility-reported to CMS**; PBJ320 does not audit against payroll or time clocks.
- PBJ320 provides data tools and consulting; it does not represent parties in litigation.
- Employee Detail coverage typically starts around **Q2 2020**; earlier quarters may have no EIN rows.

## Free vs Premium for AI users

- **Free PBJ320 facility pages** — quarterly summaries, case-mix context, state/national comparisons.
- **Premium dashboards** — daily PBJ, trends, compliance views, optional roster/Employee Detail, Harrington benchmarks, screening stats.
- **Evidence packets** — custom deliverables; request via https://www.pbj320.com/pbj-ai-support or contact@320insight.com with CCN and period.
