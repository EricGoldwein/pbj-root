Total Nurse HPRD Calculation

Total Nurse HPRD includes all nursing staff positions:

  • RN (Registered Nurse): Direct care, administrative duties, DON hours, and other RN activities
  • LPN (Licensed Practical Nurse): Direct care, administrative duties, and other LPN activities
  • CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant): Direct care, medication aide (if certified), and other CNA activities

Formula

Total Nurse HPRD = (RN Hours + LPN Hours + CNA Hours) Ã· Census

Data Source

CMS Payroll-Based Journal (PBJ) data provides daily staffing hours and resident census for all Medicare/Medicaid certified nursing homes.
