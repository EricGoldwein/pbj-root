    Forensic roster

    CMS employee-detail PBJ (where published) lists job code, work date, and reported hours per employee ID.

    Premium ties those rows to facility quarters so you can scan tenure, concentration of hours, and day-level recurrence—useful for roster verification and incident windows.

    Compliance logic

    State standards, RN minimums, and custom HPRD rules applied to CMS-reported daily totals.

    See which days fall below each rule on daily and quarterly views—built from CMS-reported PBJ hours.

    Acuity & case-mix

    Provider Information: CMS case-mix index and expected nurse staffing. PBJ: reported daily hours, aligned by quarter.

    Charts put reported staffing next to case-mix and expected metrics—acuity context from CMS data, not a clinical adequacy call.
