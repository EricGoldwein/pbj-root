About PBJ320

PBJ320 is operated by Eric Goldwein, MPH, through 320 Consulting LLC, a public-interest data consultancy focused on nursing home staffing, public health data, and evidence-based analysis.

Eric has worked across public health, journalism, nonprofit advocacy, and data analysis, with a focus on translating complex nursing home datasets into usable screening and reporting tools.

PBJ320 uses CMS Payroll-Based Journal staffing data and other public records to support screening, reporting, advocacy, and legal research. These analyses are not proof of harm, neglect, or regulatory violations on their own.

Employee roster

CMS employee-detail PBJ (where published) lists job code, work date, and reported hours per employee ID.

Premium ties those rows to facility quarters so you can scan tenure, concentration of hours, and day-level recurrence—useful for roster verification and incident windows.

Compliance checks

State reference thresholds, RN rules, and custom HPRD rules applied to CMS-reported daily totals—for screening, not a legal compliance determination.

See which days fall below each rule on daily and quarterly views—built from CMS-reported PBJ hours.

Acuity & case-mix

Provider Information: CMS case-mix index and expected nurse staffing. PBJ: reported daily hours, aligned by quarter.

Charts put reported staffing next to case-mix and expected metrics—acuity context from CMS data, not a clinical adequacy call.
