Media & Press

For journalists covering nursing homes, staffing, and ownership.

I'm Eric Goldwein, a data consultant specializing in CMS Payroll-Based Journal (PBJ) staffing data, nursing home quality metrics, and ownership structures. I've worked with PBJ data since 2019, translating complex federal datasets into clear, defensible findings for attorneys, advocates, and newsrooms.

I regularly provide background briefings, data interpretation, and rapid-turn analysis. I've appeared in media coverage, led webinars for state ombudsman programs and advocacy groups, and spoken publicly on PBJ methodology and nursing home oversight.

I'm available for both background and on-the-record reporting.

As seen in: Columbia Public Health, WTVR CBS, Positive Aging, Aging in America News.
