Press

For journalists and editors covering nursing home stories

As seen in Columbia Public Health, WTVR CBS, Positive Aging, and Aging in America News.

I'm Eric Goldwein, a PBJ data expert focused on nursing home staffing, quality, and ownership. I've worked with Payroll-Based Journal data since 2019—translating complex datasets into clear findings for attorneys, advocates, and newsrooms. I have provided background briefings, appeared in media coverage, led webinars and briefings for ombuds programs and advocates, and spoken publicly on PBJ data interpretation. I respond quickly under deadline and can support both background and on-the-record work.

How I Support Reporting

  • Background briefings — How PBJ works, what it measures, limitations and caveats. No commitment required.
  • Facility- or ownership-level analysis — Staffing trends for specific nursing homes or chains. Historical context, comparisons to state averages, regulatory compliance.
  • Trend analysis — Staffing, turnover, quality measures over time. State-level and national patterns.
  • Fact-checking and interpretation — Help verify numbers, explain methodology, clarify limitations, and correct misinterpretations. Familiar with how PBJ data is reported and how it can be misread.
  • Quotes — On-the-record or background, as appropriate for your story.
