Privacy policy

Last updated: May 2026

PBJ320 is operated by 320 Consulting LLC (we, us). This page describes how we handle information when you use the public PBJ320 website at pbj320.com, including facility pages, data summaries, contact forms, and related tools.

Information we collect

When you browse the public site, standard web server logs and similar technical records may include IP address, browser type, pages requested, and approximate timing. If you use the contact form, request a dashboard or custom report, or subscribe to updates, we receive the information you choose to submit (such as name, email, organization, and message content).

How we use information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, fulfill requested communications, and operate PBJ320. We use aggregated usage information to understand how the site is used and to maintain and improve the service. We do not sell personal information.

Public data

Staffing metrics, facility identifiers, provider attributes, and ownership listings displayed on PBJ320 are drawn from public federal and state sources, including CMS Payroll-Based Journal and related CMS provider files, as described on our data sources page. That information describes nursing homes and related organizations, not visitors to this site. Browsing or searching public facility and state pages does not mean we are collecting personal information about you from those datasets.

AI-assisted features

Some PBJ320 features may use automated or AI-assisted tools to produce summaries, takeaways, or draft reports based on public data and information you provide in context (for example, a facility name or question about a page you are viewing). Those tools are meant to help users review public data, not to replace your own verification. Inputs you submit in connection with AI-assisted features may be processed by service providers that support site operations.

Third-party services

We rely on service providers for hosting, content delivery, security monitoring, analytics, form and email delivery, and related technical operations. Some features may also use AI-assisted processing as described above. Those providers may process technical or submitted information according to their own policies and our instructions where applicable.

Premium and authenticated areas

Some dashboards, exports, or tools may require authorization or password access. Additional terms or written agreements with 320 Consulting LLC may apply to those areas. This public privacy page does not describe premium authentication or security implementation in detail.

Retention and security

We retain contact, subscription, and related operational records for as long as needed to run the service, respond to requests, and meet legal obligations. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to a public data site. No method of transmission or storage over the Internet is fully secure.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The Last updated date at the top of this page will change when we do.
