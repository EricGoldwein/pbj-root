Privacy policy

Last updated: May 2026

PBJ320 is operated by 320 Consulting LLC (“we,” “us”). This page describes how we handle information on the public PBJ320 website at pbj320.com.

Information we collect

When you use the public site, standard web server and analytics logs may record IP address, browser type, pages visited, and approximate timing. If you submit the contact form or subscribe to updates, we receive the information you provide (such as name, email, and message).

How we use information

We use contact and subscription information to respond to inquiries and send requested communications. We use aggregated analytics to understand site usage and improve the service.

Third-party services

The site may load scripts or assets from content delivery networks and analytics providers (for example, Google Analytics). Those providers may process technical data according to their own policies. Public staffing figures displayed on PBJ320 come from federal CMS public datasets described on our data sources page.

Premium and authenticated areas

Separate terms may apply to PBJ320 Premium dashboards and paid engagements. Access to facility dashboards requires authorization; we do not describe premium authentication details on this public page.

Retention and security

We retain contact and subscription records as needed to operate the service and meet legal obligations. We use reasonable technical measures appropriate to a public data site; no method of transmission over the Internet is fully secure.

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of personal information you submitted by emailing eric@320insight.com.

Children

PBJ320 is intended for professional and general adult audiences researching nursing home staffing data. It is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do.
