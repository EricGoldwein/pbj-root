Skilled Nursing Facility Change of Ownership Monitor

Track reported nursing home ownership changes and link transactions to facilities, buyers, sellers, and ownership entities.

This page tracks reported CHOW events from CMS skilled nursing facility enrollment data. A CHOW identifies a transaction or enrollment ownership change—not proof of quality problems, staffing changes, common ownership, or causation.

PBJ320 will later connect these events to staffing, citations, penalties, and ownership-control entities from CMS all-owner records. For now, use this explorer to screen transactions and follow links to facility and owner pages where available.
